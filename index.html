    The "AlertREase: A Proactive Solution for License Renewal and Penalty Management" project aims to leverage data mining, analysis, segregation, and filtering techniques to identify defaulters in Shop Establishment License, Trade License, and Labour License across different states in India. The primary objective is to raise awareness about impending license expiration and associated penalty charges among Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). Through a comprehensive notification mechanism via WhatsApp, Email, and SMS, the system ensures that MSMEs are informed one month in advance of their license expiry date, thereby minimizing the risk of default. By employing a proactive approach, " AlertREase " aims to contribute to the financial health of MSMEs, fostering compliance and preventing unintentional default.
